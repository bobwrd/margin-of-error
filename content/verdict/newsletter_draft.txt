EU AI Act — Draft High-Risk Classification Guidelines
The Commission released draft guidance to help providers and deployers decide whether an AI system is high-risk under Article 6, with feedback open until 23 June. The practical effect is earlier triage for Annex III use cases in biometrics, education, employment, migration, and justice.
First source: https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/news/commission-seeks-feedback-draft-guidelines-classification-high-risk-artificial-intelligence-systems

FTC — TAKE IT DOWN Act Enforcement Begins
The FTC started enforcing the new federal anti-deepfake law, opened a complaint portal, and warned platforms to remove nonconsensual intimate images within 48 hours of a valid request.
