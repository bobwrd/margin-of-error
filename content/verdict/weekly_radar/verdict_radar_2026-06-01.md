## Watchlist

### 2026-06-03 | Open consultation | Transparency obligations under the AI Act
The Commission’s Article 50 guidance consultation closes on 3 June. It tightens the rules on when users must be told they are dealing with AI, how synthetic content is marked, and when deepfakes must be disclosed.

First source: https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/consultations/consultation-draft-guidelines-transparency-obligations-under-ai-act

### 2026-06-23 | Open consultation | High-risk classification guidelines
Stakeholder feedback stays open until 23 June on the Commission’s draft examples for deciding when an AI system falls into the AI Act’s high-risk bucket.

First source: https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/news/commission-seeks-feedback-draft-guidelines-classification-high-risk-artificial-intelligence-systems
